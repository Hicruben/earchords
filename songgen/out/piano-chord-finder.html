FAQ

Is it free?

Yes — ChordSnap is free and runs entirely in your browser. There’s no sign-up and nothing is uploaded to a server.

How does it find the chords?

It uses on-device neural pitch detection plus harmony modelling to hear the notes and work out the chord at each moment — the same way a musician does by ear, in seconds.

Do I need the audio file?

No. You can play the song out loud, capture a tab that’s playing it, or upload a file. Whatever you use, the audio never leaves your device.
