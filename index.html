Good to know.

Clear answers before you press play.

Is ChordSnap really free?

Yes. It is unlimited and needs no account because analysis runs on your device.

What works best?

Clear guitar, piano, singer-songwriter, and practice recordings are easiest to read. Dense mixes may produce approximate chords.

Which files can I use?

MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, and OGG work—including ordinary voice memos.
